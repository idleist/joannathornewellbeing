Services

Allergy Testing

I use the kinesiology (muscle testing) method to test for allergies and intolerances. This is a non-invasive test using vials of possible allergens. The glass vials are placed on your skin (no substance is ingested or placed directly on the skin). I test for over 300 foods, drinks and additives, which covers a wide range of common consumables). With the results of the test I will provide you with detailed nutritional advice on how to safely eliminate any allergens from your diet with suitable replacements. I can also advise on weight management issues. The test is suitable for all ages.

One hour consultation £35

Acupressure

This is a form of fingertip therapy. It uses the same points used in acupuncture but pressure is applied with the fingers or thumbs instead of needles. Acupressure is performed fully clothed and can be used to treat a wide range of illnesses.

Initial one hour consultation £35.
Follow up treatments £20

Reflexology

Reflexology is another of my fingertip therapies. It works on the theory that reflexes on your feet and your hands correspond to specific areas of your body. This treatment is idea if you are feeling out of balance. Sit back, put your feet up and enjoy a renewed sense of well-being.

Cost £35 per treatment
